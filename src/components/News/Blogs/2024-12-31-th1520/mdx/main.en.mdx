On December 29, the **RevyOS** operating system for the **TH1520** completed
its kernel upgrade from **5.10** to **6.6**, and introduced updated support
for both Milk-V Meles and LicheePi 4A. The newly added support includes:

- CPU frequency scaling and fan-speed control
- Support for 8GB memory variants of Milk-V Meles and LicheePi 4A
- Proper operation on eMMC storage, with OS booting from eMMC supported
- Wired networking, allowing stable Internet access through Ethernet
- HDMI video and audio output
- Support for the AP6256 WiFi module after updating the kernel

At this point, RevyOS fully covers multiple variants of Milk-V Meles
4GB/8GB/16GB and LicheePi 4A 8GB/16GB.

###### **About TH1520**

Based on the XuanTie C910 core, TH1520 runs at up to 2.5GHz, supports
end-to-end security protection, offers 4K video processing capability, and
includes rich heterogeneous computing and high-speed I/O resources. It is well
suited to a broad range of mid- to high-performance application scenarios.
Developers are welcome to scan the QR code and note "TH1520" when joining the
WeChat group.
