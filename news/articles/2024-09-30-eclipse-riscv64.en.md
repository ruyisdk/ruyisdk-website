# Initial RISC-V Support Landed Upstream in Eclipse, and Daily Builds Are Available

Engineers from the RevyOS team at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, together with developers from other open-source communities, successfully brought initial riscv64 support into the upstream Eclipse repositories.
Eclipse can now support Java development based on OpenJDK on riscv64.
