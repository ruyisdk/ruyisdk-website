# Dr. Qiu Ji Becomes New Leader of PLCT Lab

![Qiu Ji photo](/img/newsshowcase/3.png)

Technical Director Qiu Ji will take on the responsibility of leading PLCT Lab, becoming the second team leader. Under the leadership of Dr. Qiu Ji, PLCT Lab will continue to make steady progress in areas such as compilers and virtual machines, serving as a cornerstone in China's foundational software field.
