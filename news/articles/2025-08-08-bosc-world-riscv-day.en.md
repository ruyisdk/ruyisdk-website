# PLCT Lab Participates in BOSC World RISC-V Day with Xiangshan Nanhu Laptop

On August 8, 2025, World RISC-V Days Beijing Station was successfully held at the Beijing Open Source Chip Research Institute. As the first World RISC-V Day celebration launched by the RISC-V International Foundation, this event brought together industry experts, enterprise representatives and developers from various regions to share insights and exchange cooperation, further promoting the widespread adoption and continuous innovation of RISC-V in China and globally.
