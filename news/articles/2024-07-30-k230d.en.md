# Canaan Kendryte K230D: The First Mass-Produced AIoT Chip Based on the New 32-bit RuyiSDK

Canaan Kendryte has introduced the more highly integrated K230D AIoT chip, and PLCT Lab together with the XuanTie team built a new 32-bit RuyiSDK for it.
Compared with the previous LP64-based SDK, the new 32-bit SDK reduces memory overhead by 30% and improves linked-list traversal performance by 20%.
