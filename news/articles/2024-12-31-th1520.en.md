# Kernel Upgraded from 5.10 to 6.6: New RuyiSDK Images for TH1520 Development Boards

The RevyOS operating system for the TH1520 has completed its kernel upgrade from 5.10 to 6.6 and introduced updated support for both Milk-V Meles and LicheePi 4A.
Newly added support includes CPU frequency scaling and fan-speed control, eMMC boot, wired networking, HDMI video/audio output, and the AP6256 WiFi module.
