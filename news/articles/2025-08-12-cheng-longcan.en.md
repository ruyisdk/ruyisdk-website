# Profile | Chenglong Can: Being a 'Connector' in the RISC-V World

Chenglong Can, Operations Manager at PLCT Lab, transformed from a testing team member to a community operations expert. With his passion for Linux and technical background, he is committed to making RISC-V technology visible to more people and promoting orderly community development. As a 'connector' in the RISC-V world, he builds bridges between technology and users, allowing the value of the open source ecosystem to be fully realized.
