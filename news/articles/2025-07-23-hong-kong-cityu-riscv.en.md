# RISC-V Campus Tour | PLCT Lab Visits City University of Hong Kong, Opening New Chapter in RISC-V Open Source Talent Development

On July 19, 2025, the 'RISC-V Campus Tour' event was successfully held at City University of Hong Kong, attracting nearly 100 Hong Kong university students. PLCT Lab deeply participated in this event, comprehensively presenting the lab's practices and thoughts in RISC-V software ecosystem development, talent cultivation, and industry collaboration through technical sharing, interactive exchanges, and achievement demonstrations.
