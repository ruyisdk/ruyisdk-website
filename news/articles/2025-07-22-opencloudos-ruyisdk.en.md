# OpenCloudOS Joins RuyiSDK Developer Community, Both Parties Reach Deep Cooperation

OpenCloudOS officially joins the RuyiSDK developer community, marking the beginning of deep collaboration between two major open source forces in the RISC-V field. Both parties will carry out comprehensive cooperation in operating system RISC-V architecture technology and ecosystem building, high-performance computing, and developer ecosystem construction.
